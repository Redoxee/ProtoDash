This is Air Dasher

Air Dasher is a pure platforming fun, that will test all your skills.
Tap to jump, swipe to dash, and that's it! Now you know how to play!
Try finishing the 30 levels in the least amount of time possible, featuring Ad-Free fluid gameplay!

This game is currently in alpha and is being developed by a one man team. So no worries, more levels and mechanics are coming!
